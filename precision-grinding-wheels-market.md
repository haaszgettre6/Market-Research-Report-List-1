<p><ul><li>Cylindrical External Grinding</li><li>Internal Grinding</li><li>Surface Grinding</li><li>Centreless Grinding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precision grinding wheels are categorized based on their specific applications in machining. Cylindrical external grinding focuses on the outer surfaces of parts, ensuring smooth finishes. Internal grinding targets the interior surfaces of pipes and holes for precise dimensions. Surface grinding is designed for flat surfaces, achieving high accuracy and finish quality. Centreless grinding enables the processing of cylindrical workpieces without the need for a central fixture, enhancing productivity and efficiency in mass production settings. Each type plays a critical role in meeting industry standards.</p></p>
