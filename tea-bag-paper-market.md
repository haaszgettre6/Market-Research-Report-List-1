<p><p>The tea bag paper market is primarily divided into two types: heat-sealable tea bag paper and non-heat-sealable tea bag paper. Heat-sealable tea bag paper is designed to form a hermetic seal when exposed to heat, enhancing freshness and longevity. It is commonly used for single-serving tea bags. In contrast, non-heat-sealable tea bag paper relies on adhesive or mechanical means for sealing, typically offering greater permeability and a natural feel, making it suitable for specialty teas and eco-friendly applications.</p></p>
