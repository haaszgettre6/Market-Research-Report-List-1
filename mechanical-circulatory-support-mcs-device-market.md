<p><p>The Mechanical Circulatory Support (MCS) device market consists of various types designed to assist heart function. Left Ventricular Assist Devices (LVADs) support the left ventricle, often used for heart failure patients. Right Ventricular Assist Devices (RVADs) aid the right ventricle, typically in conjunction with LVADs. Bi-Ventricular Assist Devices (BiVADs) support both ventricles simultaneously. Total Artificial Hearts (TAH) replace the entire heart for patients with severe heart failure, providing a temporary or permanent solution for advanced cardiac conditions.</p></p>
