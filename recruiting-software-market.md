<p><p>Recruiting software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based recruiting software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access it via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of updates. It typically operates on a subscription model. In contrast, on-premises recruiting software is installed directly on a company's local systems, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring more upfront investment and maintenance. Users choose based on their specific operational needs and resources.</p></p>
