<p><p>The water recycling system market encompasses various technologies designed to purify and reuse water. Reverse osmosis systems employ semi-permeable membranes to remove contaminants, ensuring high water quality. Distillation systems utilize heat to evaporate water, separating impurities. Disinfection methods, including UV light and chemical treatments, eliminate pathogens, making recycled water safe for various uses. Filtration methods, such as activated carbon and sand filters, physically remove particles and impurities, enhancing water clarity and safety. Together, these technologies contribute to sustainable water management.</p></p>
