<p><ul><li>Lumped Type</li><li>Discrete Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Fiber Raman Amplifier (FRA) utilizes the Raman scattering phenomenon to amplify optical signals in fiber-optic communication systems. The market can be segmented into Lumped Type and Discrete Type FRA. Lumped Type FRA integrates amplifier components over short distances within the fiber, providing cost-effective solutions for limited applications. Conversely, Discrete Type FRA consists of separate amplification stages, allowing for greater flexibility and scalability in high-performance communication networks. Both types enhance signal strength and extend transmission distances.</p></p>
