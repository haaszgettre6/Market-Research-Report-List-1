<p><p>The Iron-Cobalt target market can be categorized into two primary types: Plane Targets and Rotating Targets. Plane Targets are flat surfaces used in sputtering processes, allowing consistent deposition on various substrates, ideal for applications requiring uniform coatings. Conversely, Rotating Targets are mounted on a rotating mechanism, enhancing material utilization and enabling higher deposition rates. This system is particularly effective for large-scale productions, as it ensures even wear of the target material, optimizing efficiency and reducing waste in thin-film applications.</p></p>
