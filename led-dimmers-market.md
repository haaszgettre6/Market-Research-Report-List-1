Wired LED dimmers require direct electrical connections to function, providing reliable control over light intensity through physical switches. In contrast, wireless LED dimmers use remote control technology, allowing users to adjust lighting levels through smartphones or other devices without the need for complex wiring. Both types enhance energy efficiency and create customizable lighting environments, catering to residential and commercial needs while supporting modern smart home integrations.</p></p>
