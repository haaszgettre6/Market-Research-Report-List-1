<p><p>Polyurethane Reactive (PUR) adhesives are versatile bonding solutions that cure by reacting with moisture. The market includes **Non-sagging Adhesives**, designed for vertical or overhead applications, as they resist dripping and maintain shape during curing. **Self-leveling Adhesives**, on the other hand, are ideal for horizontal surfaces, spreading evenly to create smooth, uniform bonds. Both types leverage PUR's strong adhesion, durability, and resistance to environmental factors, catering to diverse industrial and construction needs while addressing specific application challenges.</p></p>
