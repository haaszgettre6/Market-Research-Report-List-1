<p><ul><li>Bolted Bonnet</li><li>Welded Bonnet</li><li>Pressure Seal Bonnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Forged steel globe valves are categorized into three main types based on their bonnet design: bolted bonnet, welded bonnet, and pressure seal bonnet. Bolted bonnet valves feature a removable top for easy maintenance, making them suitable for low to moderate pressure applications. Welded bonnet valves offer a permanent seal, enhancing reliability and minimizing leakage in high-pressure conditions. Pressure seal bonnet valves utilize a unique design that ensures a tight seal under high-pressure environments, making them ideal for critical applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs.</p></p>
