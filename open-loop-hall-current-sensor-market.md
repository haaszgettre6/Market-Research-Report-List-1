<p><p>The Open-Loop Hall Current Sensor market is divided into two main types: DC Hall Current Sensors and AC Hall Current Sensors. DC Hall Current Sensors are designed to measure direct current, providing accurate readings for applications requiring steady current flow, such as battery management and power supplies. In contrast, AC Hall Current Sensors are intended for alternating current, enabling effective monitoring of varying currents in applications like motor drives and energy management systems. Both types are essential for efficient energy monitoring and control.</p></p>
