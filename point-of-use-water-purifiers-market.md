<p><strong>The Point-of-Use Water Purifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Purifier</li><li>UV Purifier</li><li>RO Purifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Point-of-Use Water Purifiers market comprises three main types: Gravity Purifiers, UV Purifiers, and RO Purifiers. Gravity Purifiers utilize a simple filtration process, relying on gravity to draw water through filters, making them cost-effective and energy-efficient. UV Purifiers use ultraviolet light to eliminate bacteria and viruses, ensuring safe drinking water without chemicals. RO Purifiers employ a reverse osmosis membrane to remove dissolved impurities and contaminants, providing high-purity water but typically requiring electricity and maintenance. Each type caters to different user needs and budgets.</p></p>
