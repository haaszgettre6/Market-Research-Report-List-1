<p><ul><li>Inline Magnetic Flowmeters</li><li>Low Flow Magnetic Flowmeters</li><li>Insertion Magnetic Flowmeters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromagnetic flowmeter market comprises three main types: inline magnetic flowmeters, low flow magnetic flowmeters, and insertion magnetic flowmeters. Inline magnetic flowmeters are permanently installed in the pipeline, providing accurate flow measurements for various fluids. Low flow magnetic flowmeters are designed for applications with minimal flow rates, ensuring precision in smaller systems. Insertion magnetic flowmeters are partially immersed in the pipeline, allowing for easy installation and maintenance, making them versatile for various industrial applications.</p></p>
