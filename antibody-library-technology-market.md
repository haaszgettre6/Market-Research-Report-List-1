<p><strong>The Antibody Library Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phage Display</li><li>Ribosome Display</li><li>Yeast Display</li><li>Mammalian Cell Surface Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antibody library technology encompasses various display methods for selecting and optimizing antibodies for therapeutic and diagnostic use. Phage display utilizes bacteriophages to present antibodies, enabling high-throughput screening. Ribosome display relies on in vitro translation to create stable complexes of antibodies and mRNA, facilitating diverse selection. Yeast display employs genetically modified yeast cells to present antibodies on their surface. Mammalian cell surface display utilizes mammalian cells for a more physiologically relevant milieu, enhancing the selection of functional and effective antibodies.</p></p>
