<p><strong>The Pediatric Digital X-ray System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pediatric Digital X-ray System market is primarily categorized into two types: CR Tech (Computed Radiography) and DR Tech (Digital Radiography) systems. CR Tech systems use imaging plates and scanners to convert X-rays into digital images, offering a cost-effective solution for clinics. In contrast, DR Tech systems utilize flat-panel detectors to provide immediate digital images with superior image quality and faster processing times. The choice between these systems often depends on the specific needs of pediatric facilities and budget considerations.</p></p>
