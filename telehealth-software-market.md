<p><ul><li>Real-Time Communication</li><li>Store-and-Forward</li><li>Remote Patient Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telehealth software market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Real-Time Communication** allows healthcare providers and patients to interact live via video conferencing or chat, facilitating immediate consultations and decision-making.</p><p>2. **Store-and-Forward** involves collecting and transmitting patient data, such as images or medical histories, for later evaluation by healthcare professionals, enabling asynchronous consultations.</p><p>3. **Remote Patient Monitoring** utilizes devices to track patients' health metrics in real-time, allowing for continuous assessment and timely interventions without requiring in-person visits.</p></p>
