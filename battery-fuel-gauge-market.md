<p><p>The Battery Fuel Gauge market comprises several types: Discrete Gauges, Battery Management Units (BMU), and Protection Integrated Gauges. Discrete Gauges provide standalone solutions for measuring battery state-of-charge and health. Battery Management Units (BMU) offer advanced functionality, integrating monitoring, control, and communication for efficient battery usage. Protection Integrated Gauges combine fuel gauging with safety features, ensuring battery protection against overcharge and over-discharge while providing accurate state assessment. Together, these products enhance battery performance and safety across various applications.</p></p>
