<p><p>Green walls, also known as vertical gardens, are categorized into two main types: soil-less and soil-based markets. Soil-less green walls utilize hydroponic systems, where plants grow in nutrient-rich water solutions, offering lightweight and efficient options for urban settings. In contrast, soil-based green walls rely on traditional soil mediums to support plant growth, providing natural nutrients and moisture retention. Both types enhance aesthetics, improve air quality, and contribute to sustainability in urban environments, serving various design and functional needs.</p></p>
