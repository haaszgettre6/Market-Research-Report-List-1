<p><p>The Automatic Identification System (AIS) transponder market is primarily divided into Class A and Class B systems. Class A Marine AIS transponders are mandatory for larger commercial vessels, offering comprehensive tracking and communication capabilities, with high accuracy and real-time data. In contrast, Class B Marine AIS transponders are designed for smaller vessels and recreational boats, providing essential positioning and identification features at a lower cost, thus enhancing maritime safety and situational awareness without the extensive functionalities of Class A systems.</p></p>
