<p><p>The potable water pipe market is categorized into three main types: metal pipes, plastic pipes, and concrete pipes. Metal pipes, often made of steel or copper, offer durability and strength but can be prone to corrosion. Plastic pipes, such as PVC or HDPE, are lightweight, resistant to corrosion, and easy to install, making them popular for modern systems. Concrete pipes provide excellent structural integrity and are commonly used for large-scale water infrastructure projects, albeit being heavier and less flexible.</p></p>
