<p><p>The soy-based surfactants market comprises three key categories: soy flours, protein concentrates, and protein isolates. Soy flours are ground soybeans used as cost-effective surfactants, offering emulsifying properties in various applications. Protein concentrates have higher protein content than flours, enhancing surface activity and stability, ideal for personal care and industrial products. Protein isolates contain the highest protein percentage, providing superior performance and versatility in formulations, making them suitable for demanding applications in cosmetics, food, and cleaning industries.</p></p>
