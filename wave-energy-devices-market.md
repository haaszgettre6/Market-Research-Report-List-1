<p><strong>The Wave Energy Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point Absorber</li><li>Oscillating Water Columns</li><li>Oscillating Body Converters</li><li>Overtopping Wave Power Device</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wave energy devices market consists of several types: </p><p>1. **Point Absorbers** harness the energy from vertical wave motion. </p><p>2. **Oscillating Water Columns** use air pressure changes as water rises and falls. </p><p>3. **Oscillating Body Converters** capture energy from floating bodies moving with the waves. </p><p>4. **Overtopping Wave Power Devices** collect water in a reservoir as waves overtop a barrier and then generate electricity during release. </p><p>5. **Others** include various novel technologies and hybrid systems aimed at optimizing wave energy conversion.</p></p>
