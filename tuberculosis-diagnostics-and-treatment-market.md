<p><p>The Tuberculosis Diagnostics and Treatment Market encompasses various types of diagnostic methods, primarily skin testing and laboratory testing. Skin testing, such as the Mantoux test, involves intradermal administration of tuberculin to assess immune response. Laboratory testing includes techniques like sputum smear microscopy, culture, and molecular assays that confirm active infection and differentiate between latent and active TB. Effective diagnostics are crucial for appropriate treatment strategies, which may involve a combination of antibiotics tailored to the resistance patterns and patient specifics.</p></p>
