There are three main types: Acoustic Magnetic (AM) tags produce a distinctive sound when deactivated; Electromagnetic Wave (EM) tags use electromagnetic fields to trigger alarms upon unauthorized removal; and RFID tags employ radio frequency identification for tracking and inventory management. Each type offers unique benefits in enhancing security and improving operational efficiency within retail environments, ensuring product safety and reducing theft incidents.</p></p>
