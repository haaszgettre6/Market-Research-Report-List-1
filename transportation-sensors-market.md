<p><p>The Transportation Sensors market encompasses various types based on electromagnetic wave wavelengths and recording methods. Sensors operating in the UV wavelength detect short wavelengths for applications like vehicle emissions monitoring. Infrared sensors capture thermal signatures for traffic and weather analysis. Spectral sensors provide detailed material identification through broad wavelength ranges, while microwave sensors are used for object detection and tracking. Under recording methods, imaging sensors produce visual data for real-time analysis, while non-imaging sensors offer quantitative measurements for efficiency and safety.</p></p>
