<p><p>Restaurant delivery management software can be categorized into three main types: on-premise, managed, and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on a restaurant's servers, offering control over data but requiring maintenance. Managed services involve outsourcing to a third-party provider, which handles the software and infrastructure, relieving the restaurant of technical burdens. Cloud-based systems operate over the internet, allowing easy access from any device, automatic updates, and scalability, making them increasingly popular for modern restaurants.</p></p>
