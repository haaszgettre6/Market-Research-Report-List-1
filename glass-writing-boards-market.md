<p><p>The Glass Writing Boards market is categorized into two main types: Magnetic and Non-Magnetic boards. Magnetic glass boards feature a steel backing that allows the attachment of magnets, making them ideal for interactive and organizational uses in offices and classrooms. Non-Magnetic glass boards, on the other hand, lack this steel backing and are typically used for writing and presentations, providing a sleek, modern appearance. Both types offer easy cleaning and a durable, visually appealing surface for various applications.</p></p>
