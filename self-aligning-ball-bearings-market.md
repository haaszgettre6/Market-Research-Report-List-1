<p><p>Self-aligning ball bearings are a type of rolling-element bearing designed to accommodate misalignment between the shaft and housing. Their unique design features two rows of balls and a common spherical raceway in the outer ring, allowing them to adjust to slight misalignments while maintaining effective load distribution. This capability makes them ideal for applications requiring high reliability and performance, such as in automotive, industrial machinery, and agricultural equipment.</p><p>The Self-aligning Ball Bearings Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.9% during the forecast period.
