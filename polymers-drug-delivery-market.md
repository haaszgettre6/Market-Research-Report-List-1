<p><ul><li>Micelles</li><li>Microgels</li><li>Dendrimer</li><li>Hydrogels</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymers drug delivery market encompasses various types supporting targeted therapy. Micelles are surfactant-based nanoparticles that enhance drug solubility and stability. Microgels consist of cross-linked polymer networks that respond to environmental stimuli, releasing drugs on demand. Dendrimers are branched polymers offering uniformity and precise drug loading. Hydrogels are three-dimensional networks that retain water and allow controlled release. Other market types may include nanoparticle-based systems or biopolymers, each enhancing the efficacy and specificity of drug delivery in therapeutic applications.</p></p>
