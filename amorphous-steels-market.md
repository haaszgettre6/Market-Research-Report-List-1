<p><ul><li>Amorphous Ribbon</li><li>Nano-Crystalline Ribbon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The amorphous steels market is primarily categorized into two types: amorphous ribbon and nano-crystalline ribbon. Amorphous ribbons are thin, flat strips created through rapid cooling, exhibiting unique properties such as high strength and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for electrical applications and soft magnetic components. Nano-crystalline ribbons, on the other hand, consist of nanometer-sized crystalline structures that enhance magnetic performance and thermal stability. Both types are crucial for advanced technologies in electronics, energy systems, and automotive industries.</p></p>
