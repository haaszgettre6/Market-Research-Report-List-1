<p><p>The GPS locator market comprises two primary types: real-time location and regular-time location. Real-time location systems provide continuous tracking, offering immediate updates on the position of assets or individuals, ideal for applications like fleet management or personal safety. In contrast, regular-time location systems update their position at set intervals, making them suitable for scenarios where constant tracking is unnecessary, such as wildlife monitoring or event tracking. Both types cater to diverse needs across industries, enhancing operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
