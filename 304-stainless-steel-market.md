<p><ul><li>304</li><li>304L</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 304 stainless steel market includes two primary types: 304 and 304L. Both are austenitic alloys, featuring excellent corrosion resistance and good weldability. The standard 304 grade offers higher carbon content, enhancing strength, while 304L has lower carbon levels, making it ideal for applications requiring minimum carbide precipitation during welding. These properties make them suitable for various industries, including food processing, pharmaceuticals, and construction, where durability and hygiene are essential. The demand for these grades remains robust.</p></p>
