<p><ul><li>Unidirectional</li><li>Bidirectional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Barbed Sutures market is categorized into two main types: unidirectional and bidirectional sutures. Unidirectional barbed sutures feature angled barbs that allow tissue to be secured in one direction, enhancing wound closure efficiency and reducing sagging. In contrast, bidirectional barbed sutures have barbs on both sides, facilitating tissue fixation from two directions, which provides greater stability and tension distribution. Both types aim to improve surgical outcomes by minimizing tissue trauma and enhancing the efficiency of wound healing.</p></p>
