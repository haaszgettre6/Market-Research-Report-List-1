<p><ul><li>Bubble</li><li>Electronic</li><li>Ultrasonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical humidifiers market comprises three main types: bubble, electronic, and ultrasonic. Bubble humidifiers use a water reservoir, allowing air to bubble through, which adds moisture, commonly used in oxygen therapy. Electronic humidifiers employ sophisticated control systems to regulate humidity levels and are often integrated with ventilators. Ultrasonic humidifiers utilize high-frequency vibrations to produce fine mist, ensuring precise humidity control and efficiency, making them suitable for various clinical settings and patient needs.</p></p>
