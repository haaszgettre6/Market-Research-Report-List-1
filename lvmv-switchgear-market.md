<p><p>The LV/MV switchgear market comprises primarily three types: Air Insulated Switchgears (AIS), Gas Insulated Switchgears (GIS), and others. AIS utilizes air as an insulating medium and is suitable for outdoor applications, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity in design. GIS employs gas, typically SF6, for insulation, allowing for compact designs and reliability in limited spaces, making it ideal for urban environments. "Others" include hybrid technologies and products tailored for specific applications, enhancing flexibility in power distribution and management.</p></p>
