Proton Beam therapy uses charged particles to deliver high doses to tumors with minimal damage to adjacent healthy structures. Linear Accelerator machines generate high-energy X-rays for diverse cancer treatments, offering versatility in targeting various tumor types. Together, these technologies represent significant advancements in precision targeting for neurosurgery and oncology.</p></p>
