<p><p>The automotive cleaner and degreaser market is divided into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based cleaners use water as a primary solvent, making them environmentally friendly, easy to use, and safer for various surfaces. They are ideal for light to moderate dirt and grease removal. Solvent-based cleaners, on the other hand, contain chemical solvents that effectively tackle heavy grime and stubborn residue. While more powerful, they can be harsher on the environment and require careful handling. Each type serves distinct cleaning needs within the automotive sector.</p></p>
