<p><p>The Bi-Flow Dry Filter market includes two main types: Threaded Type and Pipe Welding Type. Threaded Type filters feature threaded connections, allowing for easy installation and maintenance in various applications. They are popular in situations requiring frequent disassembly. Pipe Welding Type filters, in contrast, are welded directly into piping systems, providing a seamless and robust solution for high-pressure environments. Both types are essential for ensuring efficient filtration in fluid systems, enhancing performance across various industries by maintaining clean fluid flow.</p></p>
