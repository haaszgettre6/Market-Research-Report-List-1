<p><p>Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) communication encompasses two primary market types: Cellular-Based Technology and Dedicated Short-Range Communication (DSRC). Cellular-Based Technology utilizes existing mobile networks to enable real-time communication between vehicles, allowing for broader coverage and integration with other services. On the other hand, DSRC is a short-range communication protocol specifically designed for vehicular interactions, offering low latency and high reliability for safety-critical applications. Both technologies aim to enhance road safety, traffic efficiency, and overall mobility.</p></p>
