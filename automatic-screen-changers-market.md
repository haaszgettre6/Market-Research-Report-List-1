<p><ul><li>Single Piston</li><li>Double Piston</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Screen Changers market is primarily divided into two types: Single Piston and Double Piston systems. Single Piston screen changers utilize one piston to separate and replace screens during the filtration process, offering a simple and efficient solution for low to moderate throughput applications. In contrast, Double Piston systems use two pistons, allowing for continuous operation without interrupting the production flow. This makes them ideal for high-volume processing, enhancing productivity and reducing downtime in various industrial applications.</p></p>
