<p><ul><li>Fluoropolymer PV Backsheet</li><li>Non-Fluoropolymer PV Backsheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic (PV) backsheet market is divided into two main types: fluoropolymer and non-fluoropolymer backsheets. Fluoropolymer backsheets are known for their superior chemical resistance, durability, and UV stability, making them ideal for high-performance solar panels. In contrast, non-fluoropolymer backsheets typically use materials like polyethylene or polyester, offering cost-effective solutions with acceptable performance levels but potentially lesser longevity and resilience. Both types serve key roles in protecting solar cells from environmental factors while enhancing efficiency and lifespan.</p></p>
