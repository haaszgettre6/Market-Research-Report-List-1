<p><p>The medical X-ray intensifying screen market comprises two main types: rare earth intensifying screens and calcium tungstate intensifying screens. Rare earth screens utilize materials like gadolinium oxysulphide, providing higher efficiency and reduced radiation dose, leading to enhanced image quality. Calcium tungstate screens, traditional and widely used, convert X-rays into visible light but have lower efficiency than rare earth screens. Both types play a crucial role in radiographic imaging by improving the effectiveness of X-ray film exposure.</p></p>
