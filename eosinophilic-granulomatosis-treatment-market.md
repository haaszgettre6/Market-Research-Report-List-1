Omalizumab targets IgE to alleviate allergic symptoms. Methotrexate Sodium, a traditional immunosuppressant, decreases overall immune activity. Other treatments include corticosteroids and novel agents in development, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences in managing this rare autoimmune condition.</p></p>
