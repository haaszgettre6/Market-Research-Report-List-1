<p><strong>The Intake Air Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated Into Manifold Absolute Pressure Sensor</li><li>Standalone Intake-Air Temperature Sensor</li><li>Integrated Into Mass Air Flow Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intake Air Temperature (IAT) Sensor market includes three primary types. The integrated manifold absolute pressure sensor combines pressure and temperature sensing into one unit, optimizing space and reducing cost. The standalone intake-air temperature sensor operates independently, providing precise temperature readings for engine management. Finally, the integrated mass air flow sensor merges temperature and airflow measurements, enhancing performance and efficiency. Each type plays a crucial role in automotive engine performance by ensuring optimal air density and fuel combustion.</p></p>
