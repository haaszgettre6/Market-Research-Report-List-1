<p><p>The display device market comprises several technologies that enhance visual experiences. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) uses liquid crystals to produce images and is widely used in monitors and TVs. LED (Light Emitting Diode) enhances LCD by using LEDs for backlighting, improving energy efficiency and brightness. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) features self-emitting pixels offering superior contrast and color accuracy. AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED) is a variation providing faster refresh rates and better battery efficiency. Other technologies include microLED and e-ink displays, catering to niche applications.</p></p>
