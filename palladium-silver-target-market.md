<p><p>Palladium silver targets are used in various applications, particularly in the fields of electronics and materials science. There are two primary market types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets feature a flat surface that allows for consistent deposition of material, ideal for thin-film applications. Rotating targets, on the other hand, have a cylindrical design that enhances uniformity and efficiency in material deposition by continuously exposing new surface areas as they spin. Both types cater to specific manufacturing needs in the industry.</p></p>
