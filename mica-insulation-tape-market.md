<p><ul><li>Mica Glass Tape</li><li>Mica Polyester Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mica insulation tape market is primarily segmented into two types: mica glass tape and mica polyester tape. Mica glass tape consists of woven glass fabric coated with mica, offering excellent thermal resistance and mechanical strength. It's commonly used in high-temperature applications. Mica polyester tape, on the other hand, combines polyester film with mica, providing good dielectric properties and flexibility, making it suitable for a variety of electrical insulation applications. Both types cater to different industry needs based on thermal and electrical performance.</p></p>
