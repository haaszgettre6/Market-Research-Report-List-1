<p><p>Precision cancer imaging encompasses various advanced imaging technologies essential for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) offers detailed soft tissue visualization, aiding in tumor detection and characterization. Nuclear Imaging, including PET scans, provides metabolic information about tumors, highlighting areas of abnormal activity. Ultrasound Imaging uses sound waves for real-time imaging, often guiding biopsies. Other imaging modalities may include CT scans and optical imaging, contributing to a comprehensive approach in detecting and monitoring cancer effectively.</p></p>
