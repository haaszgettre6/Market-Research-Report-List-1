<p><p>The sump pumps market comprises three main types: submersible sump pumps, pedestal sump pumps, and others. Submersible sump pumps operate underwater, effectively handling high volumes of water and reducing noise levels, making them ideal for residential basements. Pedestal sump pumps are positioned above the sump basin, offering easier maintenance and lower initial costs, suitable for smaller spaces. The "others" category includes specialized pumps like battery backup systems and sewage pumps, catering to diverse applications and specific customer needs.</p></p>
