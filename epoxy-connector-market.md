<p><strong>The Epoxy Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multi-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy connector market is segmented into single-mode and multi-mode categories. Single-mode connectors facilitate the transmission of light through a single optical fiber, allowing for long-distance communication with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunication and data centers. In contrast, multi-mode connectors support multiple light paths, enabling shorter distance connections with wider core diameters, commonly utilized in local area networks and short-range applications. Both types are essential for modern fiber optic networks and data transmission technologies.</p></p>
