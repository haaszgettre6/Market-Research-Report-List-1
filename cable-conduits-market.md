<p><p>The cable conduit market is primarily divided into two types: rigid cable conduits and flexible cable conduits. Rigid cable conduits are made from materials like PVC, metal, or fiberglass, providing strong protection and support for electrical wiring in environments that demand durability. In contrast, flexible cable conduits allow for easy installation and adaptability in spaces with complex configurations, often made from softer materials like PVC or plastic. Both types serve critical roles in safeguarding cables from environmental factors and physical damage.</p></p>
