<p><p>Tangential Flow Filtration (TFF) is a widely used membrane filtration technique that allows for the efficient separation and concentration of biomolecules, including proteins, viruses, and cells. With a growing focus on personalized medicine and vaccine production, TFF systems are being adopted to streamline downstream processing and improve product yields. These innovations, along with increased regulatory scrutiny, are shaping the future of the Tangential Flow Filtration market, making it a crucial component in the biomanufacturing ecosystem.</p></p>
