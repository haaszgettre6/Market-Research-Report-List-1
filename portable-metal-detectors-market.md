<p><p>The portable metal detectors market is primarily categorized into three types: Pulse Induction (PI), Beat Frequency Oscillation (BFO), and others. Pulse Induction detectors utilize short bursts of energy to detect metals, offering deep penetration and sensitivity, ideal for diverse environments. Beat Frequency Oscillation detectors operate by comparing frequency differences and are commonly used for shallow searches with good discrimination capabilities. Other types may include multi-frequency and very low-frequency detectors, which cater to specific user needs and applications.</p></p>
