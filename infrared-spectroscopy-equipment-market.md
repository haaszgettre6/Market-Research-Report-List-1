<p><p>The infrared spectroscopy equipment market comprises various types, including Fourier Transform Infrared Spectrometers (FTIR), Near-Infrared Spectrometers (NIR), and other specialized devices. FTIR spectrometers utilize the Fourier transform mathematical method for collecting and analyzing infrared spectra, making them highly versatile for various applications. NIR spectrometers work in the near-infrared region, ideal for rapid, non-destructive analysis, particularly in agriculture and food industries. Other equipment may include portable or handheld spectrometers, catering to niche applications and specific analytical requirements across different sectors.</p></p>
