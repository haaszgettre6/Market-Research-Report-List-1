<p><ul><li>Neutral Cure</li><li>Moisture Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modified silicone adhesive market is primarily segmented into two types: neutral cure and moisture cure adhesives. Neutral cure adhesives cure through a chemical reaction that generates no byproducts, making them ideal for sensitive substrates and environments. Moisture cure adhesives, on the other hand, cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, which allows for easier application in humid conditions. Both types are widely used in construction, automotive, and electronics due to their excellent bonding properties and durability.</p></p>
